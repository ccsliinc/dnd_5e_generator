Character Appearance
A sturdy hill dwarf with weathered tan skin and keen amber eyes that constantly scan the treeline. His brown beard is thick and well-kept, often with bits of forest debris caught in it. He moves with the quiet confidence of someone who has spent years navigating wild places, and the faint smell of pine and bear musk follows him.
Character Backstory
Thorek was a simple woodsman living on the edge of the great forest, making his living as a carpenter and guide. One fateful autumn, an orc warband descended upon his village. During the attack, he found a wounded bear cub whose mother had been slain by the orcs. Together, they fought off the raiders - Thorek with his grandfather's warhammer, and the young bear with surprising ferocity. The village elder proclaimed them both heroes, and from that day forward, Thorek and the bear - whom he named Ironjaw for his powerful bite - became inseparable companions. Now they travel the land as defenders of the common folk.
Allies & Organizations
The Village of Millhaven
The village Thorek and Ironjaw saved from the orc warband. The villagers consider him a hero and will always offer shelter and aid. He also has connections with local woodcutters and the trappers guild.
